Privacy Policy for "Abqario" App

Last Updated: April 13, 2026

Welcome to the Abqario app. We highly value our users' privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

To provide the best possible experience, we collect certain essential information when you use the app:

  • Login Information: When you sign in using your Google or Huawei account, we receive your publicly available basic information, such as your name, email address, and profile picture.
  • Gameplay and Interaction Data: We store your account level, the points you earn from answering questions, and your number of correct answers to display them within the app and update the "Leaderboard" section.
  • Device Identifiers: We may collect technical data such as device type and operating system to ensure the app functions efficiently and to send Push Notifications.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To create and manage your personal account within the app.
  • To save your game progress, level, and collected points.
  • To display the "Top 10 Leaderboard" inside the app.
  • To send important notifications and alerts regarding updates or new features.
  • To improve app performance and troubleshoot technical issues (via Firebase services).

3. Third-Party Services

The app utilizes third-party services that may collect information used to identify you. The services used in our app include:

  • Firebase: We use Firebase services (by Google) for database management (Backend), user authentication, and Cloud Messaging (notifications).
  • Google Play Services & Huawei Mobile Services: To facilitate the sign-in process.
  • AdMob Ads (Future Updates): In upcoming updates, we may integrate Google AdMob ads. This network will collect certain data to provide personalized advertisements.

4. Account and Data Deletion

We give you full control over your data. You can request the permanent deletion of your account and all associated data at any time. To do this:

  • Open the app and log in.
  • Navigate to the "My Profile" section.
  • Scroll to the bottom and tap the "Delete My Account" button.
  • Once confirmed, your account, name, email, profile picture, and all accumulated points will be immediately and permanently erased from our databases (Firebase).

5. Data Security

We value your trust in providing us with your Personal Information, and we strive to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it (such as Firebase security protocols). However, remember that no method of transmission over the internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time (for example, when integrating advertisements). We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and, if necessary, sending an in-app notification.
